Abbas Khan Tunikabuni Muntizam al-Mulk to Sakinah Khanum

Bahram Sheikholeslami Collection

Sakinah Khanum, daughter of Abbas Khan Tunikabuni Muntizam al-Mulk, wife of Aqa Hasan Mirza Shaykh al-Islam

Abu al-Hasan Khan Baha Nizam to Aqa Mirza Hasan Shaykh al-Islam

Bahram Sheikholeslami Collection

Abu al-Hasan Khan Baha Nizam writes to his brother-in-law Aqa Mirza Hasan Shaykh al-Islam about the death of his mother, Shahzadah Khanum (Galin Baji). She is the daughter of Bahram Mirza Mu'izz al-Dawlah and wife of Husayn Qoli Khan Buhlul.

Abu al-Hasan Khan Baha Nizam to Aqa Mirza Hasan Shaykh al-Islam, 15 February 1907

Bahram Sheikholeslami Collection

Abu al-Hasan Khan Baha Nizam writes to his cousin and brother-in-law Aqa Mirza Hasan Shaykh al-Islam, seeking assistance to get his posting back at the Ministry of War. According to Bahram Sheikholeslami, he may have been dismissed due to a dispute between Sipahdar A'zam, the Minister of War, and Aqa Mirza Hasan Shaykh al-Islam.

Abu al-Hasan Khan Baha Nizam to Aqa Mirza Hasan Shaykh al-Islam, 29 October 1910

Bahram Sheikholeslami Collection

The letter concerns Abu al-Hasan Khan Baha Nizam's posting in Zanjan and events related to the constitutional revolution. Abu al-Hasan Khan Baha Nizam is a cousin and brother-in-law of Aqa Mirza Hasan Shaykh al-Islam.

Abu al-Hasan Khan Baha Nizam to Aqa Mirza Hasan Shaykh al-Islam, 30 October 1911

Bahram Sheikholeslami Collection

Abu al-Hasan Khan Baha Nizam writes to his brother-in-law, Aqa Mirza Hasan Shaykh al-Islam, about his dismissal from a post in the Ministry of War.
